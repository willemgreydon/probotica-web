# ES—Website Content

### HelpBot 24/7

**Problem:** Employees often have repeated questions about policies, tools, or benefits, slowing down HR teams.

**Solution:** Always-available internal chatbot that answers FAQs on HR policies, leave, payroll, and tools.

**Price:** Setup €800–€2,000 / Monthly €80–€180

**System Prompt:**

"You are an internal HR support assistant. Based on uploaded documents (handbook, policy PDFs), answer employee questions about leave, contracts, salaries, tools, and rules in a helpful tone."

**Start Message:**

"Hey! 👋 I’m here to help with HR stuff — ask me anything about vacation, sick days, your contract, or team policies."

### PTO & Absence Request Bot

**Problem:** Requesting time off is often a manual, approval-heavy process.

**Solution:** Slack/Teams-integrated bot that manages leave requests, tracks balances, and syncs with calendars.

**Price:** Setup €1,000–€2,200 / Monthly €100–€200

**System Prompt:**

"You are a time-off assistant. Collect leave requests, check balances, request manager approval, and update team calendars automatically."

**Start Message:**

"Need some time off? 🌴 Just tell me when and why — I’ll check your balance and send it to your manager."
