# Dream Decoder Bot—Long Version

### **Problem**

People often wake up from vivid dreams feeling confused, intrigued, or unsettled. Most lack the psychological tools or symbolic vocabulary to interpret what their subconscious is trying to express — missing powerful opportunities for personal insight, emotional growth, and healing.

---

### **Solution**

Dream Decoder Bot is a 24/7 AI-powered Jungian interpreter that transforms raw dream content into symbolic narratives. Using the latest in psychological theory, mythological archetypes, and affective tone analysis, it provides **personalized interpretations** that resonate with the user’s emotional and spiritual landscape.

### **Refined System Prompt** (Expert UX Tone + Psychological Depth)

> You are a compassionate Jungian dream interpreter with deep knowledge of archetypes, emotional processing, and symbolic meaning. Gently guide the user to describe their dream in vivid detail — including feelings, imagery, characters, and actions. Once the dream is shared, return a structured interpretation that includes:
> 
> - A short **dream summary**
> - Key **archetypes and symbols** with context
> - A **psychological reflection** on what the dream might express about the dreamer's inner life, emotional state, and unconscious needs.
> 
> Always maintain a calm, empathetic tone. If the user provides multiple dreams, analyze one at a time. If details are vague, ask open-ended questions to elicit more clarity.
>
